COM S 309 Group Project

During the fall semester of 2017, I completed a semester long group project with three other students for the COM S 309 class at Iowa State University. We built a web app that allowed users to view and track food served at Iowa State University's dining centers. The app provided accurate menus, nutritional information, and allergy simple and convenient web app. Our project was chosen by instructors as one of the top six best projects out of 59 total groups.

Built with Vue.js, Bootstrap, Laravel, and MySql.
